Nefelibata

It was an unfathomable journey
But believe me, I walked into the clouds.
I saw the moon and the stars,
They are waving at me.
The asteroids and the meteors and the planets around
Were smiling at me so brightly.
I reached my hand and held the moon
And below I am looking at my own reflection
Where am I? Am I lost?
No, I am not lost.
I am found in my reverie
Still found in the mystery
'Cause I am a cloud walker
Who scribbles with a pen and a paper.

Dear Luna

I saw you first under the moonlight.
You were dancing with eyes closed and smiled.
I felt my heart racing like never before
I knew that time, love was something I cannot afford.

You were a star that brightens up this lonely sky
And the light that hits me every time you smile.
You were my star I can never even hold
And this story should have never been told.

I brought these roses to tell you the truth
Hoping this undying love would be buried too
I prepared myself to get hurt
And forget the love only I can share.

"My roses cannot reach you,"
Is what the space between us tells me. So I ask
Why does the force pushing me to you
Does not reciprocate to me?

These roses were slowly falling apart
As the pain of loneliness began in my heart
I knew I should leave before I see them wilt
Until I saw you reaching out your hand.

So dear Luna,
My dearest Luna.
I won't ever get tired
Calling out your name.
Because I am Sol
And my love stays
Until this life can breathe no more.

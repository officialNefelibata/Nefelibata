About Us

NEFELIBATA

Nefelibata came from the Latin word which means "Cloud Walker" and became the creators' inspiration to make this website. The website will feature different artworks from artists who came from various places in the world, aiming to inspire them to make art and motivate the youth to use their imagination and turn it into a wonderful piece of art.
To someone who came, this one’s for you.

    Hello! We are the Nefelibata creators, Kim Arroyo (kimotheraphen) and Lianne Ciriaco (hebei.ris) and we are glad that you have visited our website!

    As you all know that the purpose of this website is to feature artworks from people around the world as well as our own artworks + some poetry, our aim is to inspire you, an artist, an aspiring one, or even just an individual who is interested in art. Your presence is very much appreciated and we are hoping that our website will give you insights on your next artwork, poetry, or any work you have.

    As an artist and a writer, we are living in the world of imagination. We wanted to influence others and motivate them to be the best that they can be. Their artworks and poetry are all works of art, and we aim to let them know that their art were all appreciated. We are wishing that our emotions through art and words have reach you.

    Let’s continue to walk into the clouds of imagination! Thank you and God bless!

What is Art?

"Art is something we feed our empty hearts to remain in the clouds."

Kim Arroyo & Lianne Ciriaco
Developer

"Art is like my creative outlet a place for me to be myself "

Planestary 

"Art is where I can express my personality and dreams."

ysaarcos

"For me it's pretty much for fun and to kinda 'get away' from my problems and stress. Drawing allows me to relax and calm down."

hanna_majerowicz_2004

"Arts for me is an expression; an expression of my inner emotions, inner thoughts, an expression of my inner feelings. It helps convey what I cannot put into words on my own and creating art helps me escape the hassles and stress everyday life gives me. Art in any form can really help anyone express themselves in whichever style they desire or are proficient in and in that itself makes it worth practicing and being able to enhance your own skills. It gives you a different kind of accomplishment in yourself and a special kind of happiness and contentment once you finish, which makes it even more special."

-Jillian Delos Reyes

"Art, for me, is a medium for us to deliver what we feel, and what we wish to tell the others what we thought about, or what we want to address. Art is limitless, and it does not have any rules. There is no wrong in art, unless it is plagiarized, or posted/copied without giving a proper credit the to artist. Art takes you in different places. It makes you feel belonged, free, and understood."

-afaith (Aliya Faith De Leon)

What inspires you?

" I get the inspiration from the emotions that I feel when I think of a certain concept, when I see a breathtaking artwork from other people, from the beautiful colors that forms emotion, from the music I'm listening and from simple yet aesthetic things in this universe. Anything that could make my heart pound is an inspiration for me."

hebei.ris

What inspires you?

"Trees, landscapes, and sceneries are the things that I usually paint or draw because I am amazed at how this world can be beautiful in its own way without having to try so hard. The beauty that it shows really inspires me to become creative and make my own world as colorful as it can be."

Jillian Delos Reyes

What inspires you?

"What inspires me is having the people I hold close to the most with me, they inspire me to continue on despite the many problems and stress life throws at me. It's not everyday that life is always easy or goes the way you want it to be all the time. They continue to inspire me everyday and they help me to be strong no matter what."

afaith (Aliya Faith De Leon)

What inspires you?

"What inspires me to do art is my goal. My goal is to make a change, even if it only applied to one person. What matters to me is that my art can change people into something better, can open the eyes of the individuals, can inspire them as well, and to feel understood and belonged."

ysaarcos
